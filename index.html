Story
In a time when magic is a sin and users that could wield magic were casted-out as monsters, if the only person that gave you warmth and light was dragged into the depths of Hell, would you find a way to save that person? Driven by familial love, Leif lost his parents at a young age. Left with only his sister, Jade, Leif took care of her as she practiced magic growing up, only to be shunned by their very own village. Taking refuge in a cabin in the forest, Leif and Jade sought a peaceful life there. But what trials lie ahead the siblings when Jade opens the Gate to Hell and was dragged in against her will? Having lost everything he once had, Leif journeys into Hell to save his one and only sister.
