Story

Tale of the Family

In a time when magic is a sin and users that could wield magic were casted-out as monsters, if the only person that gave you warmth and light was dragged into the depths of Hell, would you find a way to save that person? Driven by familial love, Leif lost his parents at a young age. Left with only his sister, Jade, Leif took care of her as she practiced magic growing up, only to be shunned by their very own village. Taking refuge in a cabin in the forest, Leif and Jade sought a peaceful life there. But what trials lie ahead of the siblings when Jade opens the Gate to Hell and is dragged in against her will? Having lost everything he once had, Leif journeys into Hell to save his one and only sister.

Gameplay

Journey into Hell, Wielding Nothing but Magic as Your Weapon

Join Leif in his endeavor to save his sister in a challenging 2D roguelike game. Leif wields magic bullets to hone down his enemies, but deep within the culture of "Every man for himself" in Hell, what else will he learn?

  • Explore the dungeons of Hell. Each gameplay is unique, since each dungeon and map is different with each gameplay.
  • Featuring a projectile system, use magic to defeat enemies.
  • Does the concept of death exist in Hell? With each character death, the game restarts from the beginning and the entrance of Hell.
